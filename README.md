# Data_Science_Sports
The project analyzes the most watched sports in the US over time from 2004 to 2017. The data was uploaded from DataWord. Here are key takeaways: 1) The four top most watched sports in the US are: football,  basketball, baseball, and soccer.  2) According to the data, the football is the most watched sport in the US between 2004 and 2017 even though it has been on the decline since 2008 to 2017 3) The soccer popularity has been on the increase since 2007 to 2017. 4) As we see an increase in soccer and a decrease in the football over time, it is worth mentioning that the football is still the most watched sport in the North America. 5) As we see the increase in the soccer popularity in the US over time so is no interest in any sport is relatively high.
